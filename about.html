Why we exist

We couldn't find a reliable way to get hot meals or medicine after midnight, so we built our own network—trained riders, IoT-powered dispatching, and curated partners.

The MoonCart promise

Every order gets thermal-controlled packaging, live tracking, and human support. We obsess over freshness, timing, and the feeling you get when the doorbell rings.

Where we're headed

We're expanding into AI-driven forecasting so your favourites are stocked before you even add them to cart, and building micro-fulfilment hubs across Dhaka.

Radical reliability

We design backup riders, backup kitchens, and backup supply so you never feel stranded.

People-first logistics

Our couriers get wellness stipends, electric bikes, and transparent incentives.

Quality over catalog

We keep the menu tight, obsess over tasting sessions, and reject anything that isn't “wow”.

Community fuel
