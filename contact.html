Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about our services. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out to us directly.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver between 12:00 AM and 4:00 AM every day of the week. We understand that late nights can be challenging, which is why we're here to help you with your food, grocery, and medicine needs during these hours. Our service is designed specifically for night owls, students studying late, professionals working night shifts, or anyone who needs essential items during late hours.

How long does delivery take?

We strive to deliver all orders within 30 minutes of confirmation. However, delivery times may vary slightly based on your location within Bashundhara and current demand. During peak hours or adverse weather conditions, deliveries might take longer, but we'll keep you updated on your order status through our real-time tracking system.

Is there a minimum order amount?

Yes, our minimum order amount is à§³500. Orders above à§³5,000 qualify for free delivery. For orders below à§³5,000, a delivery fee of à§³60 is applied. We have structured our minimum order amount to ensure efficient service while providing value to our customers.

What areas do you cover?

Currently, we serve the entire Bashundhara Residential Area. We plan to expand to nearby areas in the near future, including Kuril, Jamuna Future Park, and Baridhara. We're constantly evaluating new areas to expand our service coverage to meet customer demands.

How do I pay for my order?

We accept secure card payments for all orders. You can pay using credit or debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through our secure payment gateway.

All online transactions are protected with industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption to ensure your financial information remains completely secure and confidential.

What if I need to cancel my order?

You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it through our app or by calling our customer service. After that, cancellation may not be possible if your order is already being processed. For order cancellations, please contact our customer support team immediately at +1 234 567 8900 or through the "Cancel Order" option in our mobile app.

If you've already paid for your order online and your cancellation is approved, we'll process a refund within 3-5 business days.

Do you deliver medicine without prescription?

For over-the-counter (OTC) medicines, no prescription is required. However, for prescription medications, we require a valid doctor's prescription which can be uploaded through our app or website when placing your order. Our pharmacist will verify the prescription before processing your order. This is to ensure your safety and compliance with healthcare regulations.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time through our mobile app or website. You'll receive notifications at key stages of your order: confirmation, preparation, and when it's out for delivery. Our tracking system provides you with the delivery person's information and estimated arrival time.
